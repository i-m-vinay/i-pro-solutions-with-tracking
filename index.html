Madrid Protocol System

What is Madrid Protocol?

The Madrid Protocol is an international treaty that allows trademark owners to seek protection in multiple countries through a single application filed with their home trademark office.

Key Advantages:

  • Single application for multiple countries
  • Centralized management and renewals
  • Cost-effective compared to direct filing
  • Streamlined prosecution process

Countries Covered (100+ Countries):

USA UK European Union China Japan Australia Canada Singapore And many more...

How long does trademark registration take?

What if my application receives an objection?

We handle objections as part of our service packages. Our legal team will prepare and file the appropriate response to overcome the objection.
